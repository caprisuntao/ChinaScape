*Identifying key friction points in the traveler experience to inform feature design.*

| Problem | Solution / Current Status |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Navigation Difficulties**: Travelers struggle to explain destinations to taxi drivers. | Prioritized "Chinese Address Flashcards" as a core requirement (FR6). |
| **Fragmented Information**: Tourist info is scattered across blogs and WeChat groups. | Developed a centralized "Core Attraction Database" requirement for the MVP. |
| **Booking Complexity**: Ticketing often requires Chinese apps or specific ID/Passport info. | Added mandatory "Passport/ID Requirements info" to attraction pages. |
